# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-Back

This repo demonstrates a monetizable Kinetic Gain Embedded primitive:

- request-time enforcement of a buyer's AI Procurement Decision Card
- enterprise AI posture that is visible at the edge, not just in review documents
- correlation-id and audit-stream traceability that can be embedded into a client platform

The hosted SaaS layer would be a managed governance bridge plus evidence packet view. The consulting layer is tenant-specific policy design, deployment mapping, and IBM watsonx integration by engagement.
